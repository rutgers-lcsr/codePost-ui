# Getting Started with codePost

## System Requirements

codePost works best on modern desktop browsers. We support the latest versions of:

- Google Chrome
- Mozilla Firefox
- Safari
- Microsoft Edge

> [!NOTE]
> Mobile browsers are not currently fully supported for the Code Console.

## Creating an Account

1. Navigate to the codePost home page.
2. Click **Sign Up** (or "Get Started").

![The signup page allows new users to create an account](/assets/docs/signup_page.png) 3. Choose your role:

### Students

If you are a student, **check your email first**. Most instructors will invite you to the course directly, which sends you a setup link.

If you have a course invite code:

1. Sign up as a **Student**.
2. Enter the invite code when prompted or in your dashboard after logging in.

### Instructors

1. Sign up as an **Instructor**.
2. You will be prompted to create your first course or organization.

3. Fill in your details (Name, Email, Password).
4. Codepost staff will validate and approve your account.
5. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox.

## Logging in

1. Go to the login page.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Click **Log In**.

### Troubleshooting

- **Forgot Password?**: Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset via email.
- **SSO**: If your university uses Single Sign-On (e.g., CAS, Shibboleth), try using your universities domain (e.g., `@rutgers.edu`) subdomains might not work.

![The login page](/assets/docs/login_page.png)

## Navigation Overview

Once logged in, you will see the main dashboard.

- **Students**: You will see a list of courses you are enrolled in.
- **Instructors/Admins**: You will see the courses you manage.

Use the sidebar or top navigation bar to access different sections like Settings or to switch between courses.
