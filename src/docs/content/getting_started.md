# Getting Started with codePost

## Creating an Account

1. Navigate to the codePost home page.
2. Click **Sign Up** (or "Get Started").
3. Choose your role:
   - **Student**: If you are taking a course that uses codePost.
   - **Instructor**: If you are teaching a course.
4. Fill in your details (Name, Email, Password).
5. Codepost staff will validate and approve your account.
6. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox.

## Logging in

1. Go to the login page.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Click **Log In**.

## Navigation Overview

Once logged in, you will see the main dashboard.

- **Students**: You will see a list of courses you are enrolled in.
- **Instructors/Admins**: You will see the courses you manage.

Use the sidebar or top navigation bar to access different sections like Settings or to switch between courses.
